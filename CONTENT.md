## ABOUT
Every Founder carries a vision. Sometimes they just need someone to help them see it more clearly.

Inspired by light, we work as strategic and creative partners, helping Founders uncover the ideas, stories and visual worlds that already exist beneath the surface, then bringing them into focus.
Through strategy, storytelling and creative direction, we help brands become more aligned, more recognisable and more deeply felt: weaving together identity, story and atmosphere to create something people want to return to.

## FOUNDER BIOS
### Swan — Creative Director
Swan is a Creative Director with a background in visual storytelling, brand identity and content direction across fashion, lifestyle and hospitality. Her work is rooted in instinct and atmosphere, shaping how a brand feels before it is fully defined. She is drawn to quiet detail, strong visual language and the emotional side of branding; the elements that sit beneath aesthetics and give a brand its depth and recognition. At SOLA, Swan leads the creative direction, translating ideas into cohesive visual worlds that feel considered, elevated and alive.
### Becks — Studio Director
Becks is a Studio Director working across brand strategy, storytelling and creative development for founders and experience led businesses. With a background spanning wellness, coaching and digital business, she brings a structured yet intuitive approach to shaping brand direction, helping clients clarify what they stand for and how that translates into language, positioning and expression. At SOLA, Becks focuses on the strategic foundation of each project, ensuring every brand has clarity, intention and a narrative that feels both aligned and commercially grounded.
